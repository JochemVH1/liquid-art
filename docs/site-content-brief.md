You are still early in the journey, but that stage is part of the story: exploration, testing, and discovery are central to the work.

## Short bio

I’ve always been a creative soul, drawn to expression in many forms — from drawing, painting, dancing, graffiti, and music to the fluid world of liquid art.

Still early in my creative journey, I’m deeply engaged in experimentation, exploring how different colors, mixtures, and flows can come together to form abstract pieces that feel expressive, layered, and visually alive.

## Longer bio

I’ve always been a creative soul, drawn to expression in many forms — from drawing, painting, dancing, graffiti, and music to the fluid world of liquid art. What began as a broad curiosity has grown into a focused exploration of color, movement, and texture.

At this stage, I’m focused on discovery — testing combinations, observing movement, and learning how each material behaves. That process allows me to create unique paintings that balance spontaneity with intention, while continuing to shape my own visual language.
